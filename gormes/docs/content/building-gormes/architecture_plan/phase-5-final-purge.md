## Relationship to Phase 6

Phase 5.F (Skills System port) is the mechanical work: porting the upstream Python skills plumbing. Phase 6 (The Learning Loop) is the algorithm on top тАФ detecting complexity, distilling patterns, scoring feedback. Phase 5.F is a dependency of Phase 6, but they are not the same work.
